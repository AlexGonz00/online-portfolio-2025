Putt Putt Park

A VR minigolf experience

In Putt Putt Park, players can play minigolf in an immerse themselves in a picturesque park setting with friends or other players online. I was primarily tasked with handling the game's functionality, where me and a team ultilized Unity's XR Interaction Toolkit along with Photon, ensuring smooth and responsive multiplayer gameplay.

Although originally created as a course submission, our team made plans to continue development on the game. This project was submitted and showcased at Texas A&M's Viz-a-GoGo31, an annual art exhibition offered by the university's Department of Visualization. The project was also showcased at ACM SIGGRAPH's 13th Annual Faculty Submitted Student Work Exhibition.
